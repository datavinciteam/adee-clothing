Born from a passion
for childhood.

Adee Clothing was born from a simple passion: to celebrate childhood through clothing. As the sister label to Fame on Central, an international high-end women's fashion retailer, we brought that same eye for quality and design to children's wear. What started as a small idea has grown into a trusted boutique brand known for thoughtfully designed baby and kids' apparel. Based in New York, with stores in Cedarhurst, Pomona, and Jerusalem, we ship our pieces to families around the world.

"Our mission is to turn every outfit into a happy memory."

Our mission is to turn every outfit into a happy memory. The way families shop for kids has changed, and we've built our brand around that shift. Parents don't want one outfit for the photo and a different one for the rest of the day. They want pieces that hold up to real life and still look beautiful when the moment matters. That's why we design thoughtful, versatile pieces, many available in matching sets so siblings can coordinate with ease, all backed by our commitment to customer satisfaction.

Clothes that move
through the whole week.

Most children's brands build collections around single events: a holiday photo, a birthday, a special occasion. We take a different approach. We treat those moments as part of a regular week, not the whole point of the wardrobe, with clothes that move easily from the playground to family events to holiday photos. Everything we make is designed to hold up to real life while still looking beautiful when it matters most. We're proud to be part of your family's happiest moments.
